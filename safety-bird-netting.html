Bird Netting Care Guide

Simple maintenance tips to ensure your bird nets stay clean and effective for many years.

Debris Removal

Occasionally shake the net gently or use a long brush to remove any trapped leaves or feathers from the mesh.

Sagging Check

Every few months, check the tension of the net. If you notice any significant sagging, give us a call for a quick retightening.

Anchor Inspection

Ensure the stainless steel hooks and wires are secure. Rust-free components mean long-term safety for your family.

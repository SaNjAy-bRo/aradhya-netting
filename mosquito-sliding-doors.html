Premium Care & Maintenance

Maintain the smooth operation and clear visibility of your mosquito doors with these tips.

Track Vacuuming

Once a month, vacuum the floor tracks to remove sand and debris. This prevents the rollers from jamming.

Mesh Dusting

Use a soft brush or a handheld vacuum to gently clean the mesh. For stubborn dust, a light wash with soapy water works best.

Roller Care

Apply a small amount of silicone-based lubricant to the roller path once a year to ensure a smooth, silent slide.

Our Story

Aradhya Netting was founded with one mission — to provide Bangalore families with modern, stylish, and reliable balcony safety solutions. Over the past 10+ years, we’ve completed more than 5000 successful installations across apartments, villas, and commercial spaces.

With a focus on safety, aesthetics, and durability, our invisible grills, anti-bird nets, sports nets, and balcony hangers have become the preferred choice for homeowners and builders alike.
